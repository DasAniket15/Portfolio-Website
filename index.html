About Me

Hey there! My name is Aniket Krish Das, and I am a passionate computer science student at KIIT University entering my final year. I possess a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic creativity. Fueled by a lifelong love of technology and a deep appreciation for storytelling, particularly through creative art forms, I have always been captivated by video games' ability to transport players into immersive fictional worlds. This fascination has ignited my pursuit of a career in game development, where I can seamlessly merge my passion for technology with my love for art and narrative.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring stories through various mediums like movies, shows, video games, and music. I find it fascinating to see how different creators use different mediums to convey their stories and bring their characters to life. I also enjoy playing the guitar and writing my own music, which has become a creative outlet for me. Furthermore, I love traveling and discovering unique places and cultures, and I enjoy capturing these journeys via photography and short films.

Aside from my creative interests, I am also an avid sports enthusiast. I have played football and basketball at a national level, and now play most sports recreationally with friends and family to stay active and refreshed. However, that does not stop me from being competitive, as my competitive spirit burns on through competitive video games like CS:GO and DOTA 2! I also partake in weight training, where I am constantly pushing myself towards a better version every single day.

Highly motivated and creative, I thrive in collaborative environments, valuing efficient teamwork and the exchange of ideas. I am constantly seeking out exciting opportunities that allow me to contribute significantly while collaborating with talented and diverse individuals.

Enthusiastic about the future, I am eager to continue learning and expanding my skill set in all aspects of my life!
