About Me

Hey there! My name is Aniket Krish Das, and I am currently a pre-final year student pursuing computer science at KIIT University. I have always had a passion for technology, which is complemented by my love for creative art forms, especially storytelling. I have always been fascinated by the power of video games to immerse players in a fictional world and engage them in a story. This has led me to pursue a career in game development, where I hope to merge my passion for technology with my love for art and storytelling.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring stories through various mediums like movies, shows, video games, and music. I find it fascinating to see how different creators use different mediums to convey their stories and bring their characters to life. I also enjoy playing the guitar and writing my own music, which has become a creative outlet for me. Furthermore, I love traveling and discovering unique places and cultures, and I enjoy capturing these journeys via photography and short films.

Aside from my creative interests, I am also an avid sports enthusiast. I have played football and basketball at a national level, and now play most sports recreationally with friends and family to stay active and refreshed. However, that does not stop me from being competitive, as my competitive spirit burns on through competitive video games like CS:GO and DOTA 2!

Overall, I am a driven and creative individual with a passion for both technology and storytelling, who also values efficient teamwork. You can find me always looking for exciting opportunities which will enable me to not only contribute a great deal but also meet a variety of unique and talented individuals.

I am excited to see where this journey takes me, and I am eager to continue learning and growing in all aspects of my life!
