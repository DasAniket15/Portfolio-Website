About Me

Hey there! I am Aniket Krish Das, currently a computer science sophomore at KIIT. I hope to merge my love for technology and my passion for thinking creatively, and become a game designer! I value efficient teamwork, and am always eager to learn something new. I am always looking for great opportunities which will enable me to not only contribute a great deal, but also meet new and interesting people.

Coming to hobbies, I enjoy consuming a well developed story via reading a book, watching a movie, or playing a video game. I love listening to music, and play the guitar as well. I love travelling and discovering unique places and cultures. I enjoy capturing these journeys via photography and short films. I am also quite competitive when it comes to sports. Having played football and basketball at a national level when I was younger, I now play most sports recreationally. However, my competitive spirit burns on through competitive games like CS:GO and DOTA 2!
